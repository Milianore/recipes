Pumpkin Cake

Description

Cozy up with this pumpkin cake recipe. Itâ€™s sweet, subtly spicy, and wonderfully light and fluffy.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Leaveners
  • Cinnamon
  • Salt
  • Sugar
  • Oil
  • Pumpkin
  • Vanilla
  • Eggs
  • Nuts

Steps

  1. Mix the dry ingredients in one bowl and beat the wet ingredients in another.
  2. Beat the flour mixture into the wet mixture.
  3. Stir in the nuts (if using) and pour it into a prepared sheet pan.
  4. Bake until a toothpick comes out clean.
