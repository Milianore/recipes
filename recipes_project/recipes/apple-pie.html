Apple Pie

Description

Get a jump start on fall baking season with this apple pie filling recipe. It makes enough delicious filling for 5 pies, so itâ€™s perfect for freezing.

Ingredients

  • Apples
  • Lemon juice
  • Water
  • Sugar
  • Cornstarch
  • Salt
  • Spices

Steps

  1. Toss the apple slices with lemon juice.
  2. Combine the remaining ingredients in a Dutch oven and bring to a boil.
  3. Add apples, return to a boil, then simmer until the apples are tender.
  4. Freeze according to the recipe.
